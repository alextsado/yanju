Tips on creating a project post

  1. Provide 2 things: brief background on your company, and few sentences on the problem
  2. Your problem/ project goal should be clear: it should be clear what you hope to get out of 1 hour
  3. Start working on your problem on your own, so the 1 hour is spent with you asking questions, instead of starting from scratch
  4. Title of post should be less than 6 words, and suggest the goal of the project post
